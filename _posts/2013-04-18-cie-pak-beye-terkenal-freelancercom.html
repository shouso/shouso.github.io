Cie... Pak BEYE emang menggebrak dunia pertuiteran nih... sampe-sampe situs freelancer.com bikin kompetisi untuk ngerancang tampilan twitternya pak BEYE sebagai bentuk sambutannya...

Pengumuman lengkapnya kek gini..

Twitter @SBYudhoyono Indonesian President Design Contest #Presidentwit

 

Contest Brief

Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono, President of Indonesia since 2004 and widely known as "SBY", has joined twitterverse (https://twitter.com/SBYudhoyono). Tweeting since 13 April 2013, he now has more than a million followers; well ahead of his fellow world leaders: Australia's Julia Gillard (370k followers) and India's Dr Manmohan Singh (500k followers).

In welcoming SBY to Twitter, Freelancer.com is hosting an unofficial contest to design his twitter account: profile pic or logo, header and background.
As he is the main representative of Indonesia, we are looking for something that will appeal to the eyes of international audience and also highlights the world's 4th most populated country.

Design entries must include:
1. A profile picture or logo to represent SBY for his new Twitter account https://twitter.com/SBYudhoyono.
2. A header design.
3. A background design.

To increase your success rate, post your design and share it on twitter:
http://tinyurl.com/SBYudhoyonoContest

Note: You don't have to use the attached files. Show off your creativity!

File types:

You'll need to provide your design in the following file type(s): AI,EPS"
